A Ralph Moody Family Album

The Moody family's story begins with the book Little Britches, right after they move to Colorado to try their hand at ranching. One quickly becomes endeared to Ralph's father, Charles.  In this book Ralph gets his nickname of "Little Britches".  As the series continues, you become acquainted with all of Ralph's family, and they are some of the best friends that you never met!

In Man of the Family and Mary Emma & Company, you respect the strength of Ralph's mother, Mary Emma.  In The Fields of Home you meet Ralph's grandfather, Thomas J. Gould, and Ralph's great-uncle, Levi Gould.

The Dry Divide takes place in Kansas and Nebraska, where you learn about harvesting and threshing wheat, and also about a unique harvesting crew. You cannot start the series without finishing it, and you might even catch yourself saying "By fire, we;ll show them what kind of logs makes wide shingles" or "Tarnal fool yella colt," but if you talk too much like that people will get to thinking you are a "horse of a different color!"  Would you like to know more about Ralph and his brothers and sisters? The Little Britches series is now in print again, so you may have these wonderful books in your personal library.
