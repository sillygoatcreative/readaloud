Little Britches Books and Links

The books in the Little Britches series are:

    Little Britches: Father and I Were Ranchers (1950)
    Man of the Family (1951)
    The Home Ranch (1956)
    Mary Emma & Company (1961)
    The Fields of Home (1953)
    Shaking the Nickel Bush (1962)
    The Dry Divide (1963)
    Horse of a Different Color: Reminiscenses of a Kansas Drover (1968)

A few variations of the Little Britches series:

    Little Britches: Man of the Family (1953, an abridged edition of the two books)
    The Wild Country (1970, formerly published as Little Britches,
                                    along with a Disney movie of the same name, starring Ron Howard!)

More books by Ralph Moody:

    Kit Carson and the Wild Frontier (1955)
    Riders of the Pony Express (1958)
    Geronimo, Wolf of the Warpath (1958)
    Silver and Lead: the Birth and Death of a Mining Town (1961)
    Wells Fargo (1961)
    American Horses (1962)
    Come On Seabiscuit (1963)
    The Old Trails West (1963)
    Gateways to the Northwest (1963)
    The Valley of the Moon (1966, a play)
    Stagecoach West (1967)
