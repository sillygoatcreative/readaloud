A Visit with Phil Moody

On July 1, 1998 my husband, three children and I had a wonderful outing with Phil Moody. We visited his New England home and viewed family photographs and paintings, while Phil reiterated some Moody family history. Phil Moody reminded me of Ralph --- although I only knew Ralph from his books. Phil was a great storyteller and extremely sharp for his 96 years. He was also quite sweet; a highlight of this memorable day was when Phil took our family out for a Chinese food luncheon.  He also guided us through Medford, Massachusetts to point out the original "Mary Emma" house.
