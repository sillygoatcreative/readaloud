Presenting -- Harpo Speaks!

                              by Harpo Marx with Rowland Barber, copyright 1961

           Harpo Speaks! , 475 pages from the quietest Marx Brother, is an
           absorbing tale of Harpo's charmed life. His journey from NY's
           upper east side (Harpo was thrown out of school for the last time
           at 8 years of age) through Vaudeville to Broadway and Hollywood
           are well documented. However, Harpo Speaks! is so much more ...
           it's an intimate insight into what made Harpo, Harpo.
                In Harpo Speaks! you will discover: how to make a Chico-
                   proof watch ... the origin of the "gookie" ... how the harp

            came to Harpo ... Alexander Woolcott and the Algonquin
           Round Table ... a passion for croquet ... murder mystery
      dinner parties ... Harpo's 1933 tour of Russia and secret mission ...
     the Hollywood Round Table with Jack Benny, George Burns, Eddie
     Cantor and Groucho ... life on a Harp Ranch ... and even how to play
     Pinchie Winchie!
