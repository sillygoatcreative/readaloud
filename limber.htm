Here is a classic story which I was drawn to because I collect violin books. It was more than I bargained for, however, since it included romance and a heroine who worked her way through school by selling moths to collectors. I have since met several Gene Stratton Porter fans who said the book Freckles inspired a lifetime love of insects and nature.
